HOW TO GET SUPABASE ANON KEY:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Go to: https://supabase.com/dashboard
2. Sign in to your account
3. Select your project (or create one)
4. Click "Settings" (gear icon) → "API"
5. Under "Project API keys", find "anon" or "public"
6. Click "Reveal" to show the full key
7. Copy the ENTIRE key (it's very long, 100+ characters)
8. Paste it as the value for NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY
